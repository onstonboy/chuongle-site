# Subkit

# landing page url: subkit.chuongle.dev

## The most beautiful way to see where your money goes

**Subkit** is a subscription tracker for people who juggle many recurring charges—streaming, music, apps, fitness, cloud storage, and more. It shows your total monthly spend at a glance, surfaces when the next bill hits, and reframes recurring costs with tasteful visuals. Manual entry only: no bank linking, no transaction import—just clarity and control on your phone.

## Key Features

- **Dashboard at a glance** — Hero monthly total, subscription cards with brand icons, list or grid layout, and an upcoming-charges timeline with gentle emphasis when a bill is due soon
- **Fast add flow** — Add a typical subscription in under five seconds: name, price, billing cycle, next bill date, and visual identity from a curated brand set
- **Flexible billing** — Monthly, yearly, or custom intervals in days; monthly equivalent for apples-to-apples totals; mark paid and adjust dates so your timeline stays honest
- **Gestures and control** — Swipe for edit and delete, long-press reorder, and smooth motion tuned for a premium feel
- **Insights and equivalents** — Hero insight slides, charts, and pain-meter style equivalents (e.g. coffees) for emotional awareness—full chart set on Premium
- **Widgets (Premium)** — Home screen widgets for spend pulse, next renewal, timeline, equivalents, and top costs; tap deep links back into the app
- **Privacy-first** — Subscription records stored on-device; works offline; localized for multiple languages
- **Free and Premium** — Free tracks up to five subscriptions; Premium unlocks unlimited subs, widgets, themes, and full dashboard insights via in-app purchase with restore

## ✨ Perfect for

Gen Z and young professionals who want aesthetic UI, smooth motion, and a clear picture of recurring spend—without corporate finance software or bank logins.
