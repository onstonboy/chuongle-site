## 🚀 What is AI Caption?

**AI Caption** is the ultimate AI-powered mobile app that transforms your photos into captivating social media content. Powered by Google's advanced Gemini AI, our app automatically generates creative, engaging captions tailored to your images, mood, and style preferences.

Never struggle with writer's block again! Whether you're sharing a stunning sunset, a delicious meal, your pet's adorable moment, or a special celebration, AI Caption creates the perfect words to match your photos.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎯 **Smart Caption Generation**
- **AI-Powered Analysis**: Automatically analyzes your images and generates contextually relevant captions
- **Multiple Genres**: Choose from 20+ caption genres including:
  - Romantic, Inspirational, Poetic, Trendy, Lifestyle, Food, Adventure, and more
- **Customizable Tones**: Express yourself with tones like:
  - Casual, Humorous, Reflective, Sarcastic, Genuine, Flirty, Energetic, and many others
- **Length Options**: Generate short, medium, or detailed captions to fit any platform
- **Hashtag Support**: Automatically generate relevant hashtags for maximum reach

### 🌍 **50+ Languages Supported**
Generate captions in over 50 languages including:
- English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese
- Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean
- Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Vietnamese, and many more!

### 🎨 **State-Based Captions**
Express your current mood with state-based caption generation:
- Happy, Loved, Cool, Chill, Excited, Relaxed
- Sad, Tired, Angry, Frustrated, Nervous, Lonely
- And many more emotional states!

### 📸 **Image Rating & Analysis**
- **Smart Image Rating**: Get AI-powered scores and feedback on your photos
- **Category Classification**: Automatically categorizes your images (Nature, Portraits, Food, Architecture, etc.)
- **Style Analysis**: Identifies photo styles (Minimalist, Cinematic, Vintage, HDR, etc.)
- **Improvement Suggestions**: Receive actionable tips to enhance your photography

- **Background Removal**: Instantly remove backgrounds from your images
- **Text on Image**: Add beautiful text overlays with customizable fonts and colors
- **Collage Creator**: Create stunning photo collages
- **Style Filters**: Apply various artistic filters and effects
